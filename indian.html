Indian Cuisine

Palak Paneer

Ingredients:

500g fresh spinach leaves
250g paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cubed
1 small onion, chopped
2-3 cloves garlic, minced
1-inch piece of ginger, minced
2-3 green chilies, chopped
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 teaspoon cumin seeds
2 medium tomatoes, chopped
1 teaspoon ground turmeric,coriander,garam masala
1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
Salt, to taste


Recipe:

Blanch spinach leaves, then blend with onion, garlic, ginger, and green chilies to make a puree.
Fry paneer cubes until golden brown. Set aside.
In a pot, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Sauté chopped onions until translucent.
Add chopped tomatoes, cook until mushy, then add spices (turmeric, coriander, garam masala, red chili powder, salt).
Pour in spinach puree, simmer for 5-7 minutes.
Add fried paneer cubes, mix gently, and simmer for a few more minutes.
Adjust seasoning if needed.
Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
